Privacy Policy — Fit & Fly

Rhinegold Studio · Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy describes how Rhinegold Studio ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the Fit & Fly mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Controller

Alexander Fuchs · Rhinegold Studio

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Game progress (completed levels, star ratings, scores)
  • Audio preferences (sound effects and music on/off)

This data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising — Appodeal

Fit & Fly shows optional rewarded video advertisements via Appodeal. Ads appear only when you choose to watch one in exchange for a hint. You are never shown ads without actively choosing to do so.

When you first launch the app, you may be shown an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking permission to use your advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalised ads. If you decline, contextual (non-personalised) ads are served instead. Either way, the app functions identically and hints remain available.

Appodeal is a mobile ad mediation platform that connects multiple ad networks. Appodeal and its partner networks may collect the following data to serve and measure ads:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), if ATT consent is granted
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Coarse location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, completions, clicks)
  • IP address

The legal basis for this processing is your consent (via the ATT prompt) or our legitimate interest in monetising the app to support its development (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for non-personalised ads.

Appodeal is provided by Appodeal Inc., McLean, Virginia, USA. For more information, see the Appodeal Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics — Firebase Analytics

Fit & Fly uses Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game and to improve the experience. Firebase Analytics collects:

  • Anonymous app usage events (e.g. app open, session duration)
  • Device information (device model, OS version, language)
  • Anonymised installation and session identifiers

Firebase Analytics data is anonymised and aggregated. It is not used for advertising purposes.

Firebase Analytics is provided by Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA. Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. For more information, see the Firebase Privacy Policy.

The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interest in improving the app (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

5. Apple App Store

The App is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at apple.com/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Fit & Fly is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user. Appodeal is configured to serve age-appropriate ads only. Firebase Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise these rights, please contact contact@rhinegoldstudio.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The competent authority for Baden-Württemberg is the LfDI BW: www.baden-wuerttemberg.datenschutz.de.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when new features are added or legal requirements change. The current version is always available at this URL. Material changes will be communicated via an app update.
