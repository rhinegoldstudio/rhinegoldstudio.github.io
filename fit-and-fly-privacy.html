Privacy Policy — Fit & Fly

Rhinegold Studio · Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy describes how Rhinegold Studio (“we”, “us”) handles information in connection with the Fit & Fly mobile application (“the App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Controller

Alexander Fuchs · Rhinegold Studio
contact@rhinegoldstudio.com
rhinegoldstudio.com

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Game progress (completed levels, star ratings, scores)
  • Audio preferences (sound effects and music on/off)

This data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising — Chartboost

Fit & Fly shows optional rewarded video advertisements via Chartboost. Ads appear only when you choose to watch one in exchange for a hint. You are never shown ads without actively choosing to do so.

Fit & Fly is rated 4+ on the App Store. In accordance with Apple’s guidelines for apps rated 4+, no App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt is shown and no advertising identifier (IDFA) is collected. Chartboost serves contextual (non-personalised) ads only.

Chartboost is provided by Chartboost, Inc., 22 4th Street, San Francisco, CA 94103, USA. Chartboost may collect the following data to serve and measure ads:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Coarse location (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, completions)
  • IP address

The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interest in monetising the app to support its development (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

For more information, see the Chartboost Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics — PostHog

Fit & Fly uses PostHog for anonymous product analytics to understand how players interact with the game and to improve the experience. PostHog collects anonymous event data including:

  • Level start, completion, and timeout events
  • Hint usage events
  • Audio preference toggles
  • Anonymised device and session identifiers

PostHog analytics data is fully anonymised and cannot be linked to any individual. No opt-out mechanism is required as no personal data is processed.

Data is processed by PostHog, Inc. and stored on servers in the United States. For more information, see the PostHog Privacy Policy.

5. Apple App Store

The App is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at apple.com/privacy.

6. Children’s Privacy

Fit & Fly is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user. Chartboost is configured to serve COPPA-compliant, non-personalised ads only. PostHog analytics do not collect personally identifiable information.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise these rights, please contact contact@rhinegoldstudio.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The competent authority for Baden-Württemberg is the LfDI BW: www.baden-wuerttemberg.datenschutz.de.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when new features are added or legal requirements change. The current version is always available at this URL. Material changes will be communicated via an app update.
