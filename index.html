About

Crafted with curiosity & care

rhinegold studio is an independent one-person studio building mobile games and apps that are worth your time. Inspired by the depth and ambition of Wagner's Das Rheingold, I believe small studios can create experiences that are genuinely surprising — from puzzle games that challenge your thinking to apps that help you discover something new. I don't chase trends. I build things I'd love to play myself.
